Milestones

I was an active member of the electronics interest group from where I developed a keen interest in robotics which drove me towards pushing the Execom to form a robotics and automation society chapter in order to give better opportunities to students who have a lot of interest in robotics but doesn't have a platform for it within the SB, So with the help and guidance from my seniors and colleagues, I took the initiative to set up a RAS chapter in our SB which will help students to boost up their knowledge. As part of the RAS setup, I along with my team developed robotics and Automation interest group which helped the students to know more about this fascinating field. Meetings are conducted weekly to clarify their doubts and for discussions. In November,2020 we received the approval from IEEE to form the RAS Chapter in our college.

Got selected to the Rover club of RSET, Which had an MoU with Frankfurt University, Germany. Our Team collaborated with the German team to develop an autonomous Martian rover to be submitted at the International rover competition. Received an offer for a funded visit to Frankfurt university.
